Land O Lakes 










Welcome to the original J. Joseph Salon. We’re located in the Dupree Plaza at the intersection of Dupree Drive and Land O’Lakes Boulevard. This salon location houses high level stylists who specialize and teach innovative haircuts, advanced hair color techniques, revitalizing hair highlights and lowlights, as well as hair extensions to all J Joseph stylists. If there’s any way we can assist you, please contact us and one of our front desk team members will promptly attend you.
