Curly Hair Experience 

Discover a transformative service designed to elevate your curls to their full potential. At J. Joseph Salon, our expert stylists specialize in delivering personalized experiences tailored to your unique curl pattern and hair’s specific needs. With extensive knowledge of shapes, angles, and diverse hair textures, our team creates customized hairstyles that complement your individuality. 



Request Appointment 







Curly Hair Experience – A Signature Service by Certified Curl Specialists 

What to expect 

Your journey begins with a personalized consultation to understand your vision and hair goals. Our curl specialists will then provide an exceptional shampoo and conditioning treatment, using products selected to suit your texture and enhance your curls. 

ENHANCE YOUR SERVICE 

Elevate your experience with optional add-ons:

An extended scalp massage with a customized deep conditioner. 
A warm towel infused with essential oils for ultimate relaxation.
A luxurious steam treatment to boost moisture absorption, leaving your curls soft, defined, and bouncy (additional charge applies). 

Booking Your Curly Hair Experience 

To reserve your Curly Hair Experience, you can book an appointment with one of our select JJS Curly Hair Specialists or Certified Experts. A $50 deposit is required at the time of booking, which will be applied toward the total cost of your service. Our standard cancellation policy applies: 

Deposits are refundable with at least 24 hours' notice of cancellation. 
If rescheduling, your deposit will be applied to the new appointment, provided you reschedule at least 24 hours in advance. 



Request Appointment 







*All curly haircuts require a deposit at booking: $25 with a Curly Cut Specialist and $50 with an Ouidad-Certified Specialist. * Other restrictions may apply.
