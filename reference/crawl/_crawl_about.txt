About 

Who We Are 

J. Joseph Salon is a group of upscale hair salons located in Pasco and Hillsborough County, Florida. We are owned and operated by hairstylists who dream big and go after that dream every day. All of our stylists were handpicked for the masterful skills, artistry and professionalism they display every time they’re behind a chair. We thank you for trusting us to care for you and allowing us to offer you our most professional recommendations. J. Joseph Salon is an elite Redken Salon which means we offer Redken color services and the full line of Redken and Pureology hair care and styling products to all of our guests.

Our Mission 

Our mission is called our Standard of Excellence. We strive to go beyond the norm for salons. We want to be distinguishable, and excellent and that begins with our guests in mind. Each of our stylists has gone through our JJS advanced education program that ensures a high-level of technicality, artistry and quality for every guest at our salons. Our salons have gained global recognition because of our commitment to upholding our Standards of Excellence every day. We thank you, our guests, for allowing us to give you our very best. Your satisfaction is our number one priority and we want our professional skill sets to meet  your needs.

Our Culture 

Over the years J. Joseph Salons have become distinguished by the level of excellence we bring to not only our guests but also to our stylists. The ecosystem within our salons is driven by our family dynamic and unified life objectives. To all of our new stylists, we proudly welcome you to the family.
